You know, I would have loved to have lived the life of Jack Kerouac.  People keep calling me a hippie, but I like to think of myself more as a Beatnik.  When I lived in San Francisco, I would compulsively go to City Lights Bookstore, and buy books.   Hell, I still go back there and spend literally hundreds of dollars every time I'm even near SF. 

I watched this, and this overwhelming urge to have a Camel and a highball came over me.

If you've never read it, you should go out and buy "On the Road"
